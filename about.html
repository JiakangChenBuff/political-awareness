Who We Are
Rhapsody for Youth is a high school student-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.
Our mission is to interact with young individuals by motivating them to articulate their ideas and self-advocate for positive changes in their lives by providing services, such as classes, seminars, workshops, podcasts, a YouTube channel, and volunteering opportunities. We believe it is important to engage the youth in thoughtful conversations and discussions. Rhapsody for Youth is to make us heard.
