'Double Mutant' Covid-19 Variant (of India)
Up to now, India is documented to have a 1.9% vaccination rate. This proved to be devastating news for India when the first strain of B.1.617 was uncovered in Maharashtra, India on October 5, 2020. B.1.617, sometimes referred to as the ‘Double Mutant,’ is a novel variant of Covid-19. In the media, B.1.617 dubbed as the ‘Double Mutant,’ because it has developed into two more main types of mutations. However, while there are two main types, there are a sum of 13 smaller mutations as of now.
