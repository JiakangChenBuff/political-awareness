What we do

Engage for Change is a news organization that creates content specifically for young people.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate young people about the world around them in order to encourage engaged citizenship and promote youth empowerment.

Engage for Change is run by Rhapsody 4 Youth, a high school student-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Our mission is to interact with young individuals by motivating them to articulate their ideas and self-advocate for positive changes in their lives. Learn More
